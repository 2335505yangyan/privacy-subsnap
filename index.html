# Privacy Policy for SubSnap **Last updated: March 27, 2026** SubSnap ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our "Local-First" approach to your data. --- ## 1. Local-First Data Philosophy SubSnap is designed to be a private tool. **We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal subscription data to any external servers.** - **No Account Required:** You can use all features without registration. - **Local Storage:** All subscription names, prices, and dates you enter are stored **exclusively on your device**. - **No Tracking:** We do not use any third-party analytics or behavioral tracking tools. --- ## 2. Permissions & Data Usage - **Notifications:** SubSnap uses **local notifications** to remind you of upcoming payments. These are generated entirely on your device and are not sent externally. - **Internet Access:** Network access is used **solely** for processing In-App Purchases via the Google Play Billing system. Your financial details (credit card, etc.) are handled by Google and never touched by SubSnap. --- ## 3. Data Responsibility Since SubSnap does not use cloud sync, your data's safety depends on your device. - If you delete the app or lose your device without a manual backup (if supported), your data will be permanently lost. - We have no way to recover your data because we never had access to it. --- ## 4. Third-Party Services SubSnap is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain non-identifying technical information (such as device model or crash reports) to ensure app stability and manage payments. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for more details. --- ## 5.
