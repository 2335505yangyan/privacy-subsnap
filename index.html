Privacy Policy for SubSnap Last updated: March 27, 2026 Overview SubSnap is developed by YukiMind. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how SubSnap handles your data. Data Collection SubSnap does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including subscription names, amounts, and payment dates — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Data Storage All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers, do not require account registration, and do not have access to any information you enter into the app. Notifications SubSnap uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming payments. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers. Third-Party Services SubSnap uses Google Play Billing for the one-time Pro purchase. This transaction is handled entirely by Google. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about how Google handles payment data. Children's Privacy SubSnap does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy occasionally.
